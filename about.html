Our Story 📖

Woohoo is India's largest gift card marketplace, built by Pine Labs — one of Asia's leading fintech companies. We started with a simple belief: gifting should be instant, personal, and hassle-free.

Traditional gifting is broken. You spend hours guessing what someone wants, buy something they might return, and wrap it in paper that ends up in the trash. Gift cards fix all of this — they let the recipient choose exactly what they want, while you get the joy of giving.

Today, Woohoo connects millions of Indians with 300+ brands across fashion, food, travel, entertainment, electronics, beauty, and more. Whether it's a birthday, a wedding, Diwali, or just a "thank you" — we make every moment giftable.

Our Mission 🎯

To make every celebration in India more joyful by giving people the power to gift smarter. We believe in a world where no gift goes unwanted, no occasion goes uncelebrated, and no moment of appreciation goes unexpressed.

Powered by Pine Labs 🌲

Pine Labs is one of Asia's leading merchant commerce platforms, serving over 150,000 merchants across India, Malaysia, and the Middle East. As a Pine Labs company, Woohoo leverages world-class technology, security infrastructure, and payment expertise to deliver the most trusted gift card experience in India.
